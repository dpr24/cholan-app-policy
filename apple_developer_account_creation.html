Apple Developer Program
Enrollment Guide

A complete step-by-step walkthrough for enrolling in the Apple Developer Program — from sign-in to subscription.

📱 Apple Developer App ✅ Identity Verified 🏢 Organizations Supported

Enrollment Steps

1

Navigate to developer.apple.com

Open your preferred web browser and go to developer.apple.com. Click the Account button to sign in with your Apple ID. If you don't have one, click Create yours now, enter your details, and complete the Apple ID setup. Once signed in, click Enroll Now to begin.

2

Download & Set Up the Apple Developer App

The Enroll Now page links you to the Apple Developer App on the App Store. Download, install, and open it. Sign in with your Apple ID via the Account tab, then tap Enroll Now. Review the summary of benefits and requirements carefully before tapping Continue.

3

Enter Personal & Identity Information

Provide your legal first name, last name, and phone number. The name must match exactly as it appears on official identification — no nicknames or company names allowed. Complete identity verification using a government-issued photo ID such as a driver's license. Capture a clear photo and tap Confirm.

⚠️ Note for Mexico: Driver's licenses are not accepted for identity verification. An alternate government-issued ID is required.
4

Provide Organization Details

Select your entity type and enter the legal entity name (no CBAs or trade names — this name appears as the seller in the App Store). Enter your D-U-N-S number (a unique 9-digit identifier by Dun & Bradstreet); if you don't have one, request it at no cost. Also provide the organization's headquarters address, phone number, and an active public website domain.

5

Authority Confirmation & Verification Contact

Confirm that you have legal authority to bind the organization to Apple's agreements. Provide a contact person who can verify this authority. Submit all information and monitor your email for Apple's review response and any follow-up requests.

💡 Nonprofits, educational institutions, and government organizations may qualify for a fee waiver. Request it via the provided link during enrollment.
6

Finalize Enrollment & Subscribe

Once Apple approves your application, you'll receive an email. Reopen the Apple Developer App, tap the Account tab, and sign in if needed. Tap Continue Your Enrollment, then review the Apple Developer Program License Agreement carefully. Tap Agree, review the annual membership details, then tap Subscribe to complete enrollment.

Fee Waiver — Detailed Information

🎓 Annual Fee: $99 USD/year — Waived for Qualifying Organizations

Apple offers a full fee waiver for the $99 annual Apple Developer Program membership to eligible nonprofit, educational, and government organizations. The waiver is confirmed active in 2026.

Who Qualifies?

🤝 Registered Nonprofits
🎓 Accredited Educational Institutions
🏛️ Government Entities
👤 Individuals / Sole Proprietors

Conditions to Maintain the Waiver

🆓All apps under the account must be free — no paid apps or in-app purchases allowed.
📄You must not have signed the Paid Applications Agreement (Schedules 2 and 3).
🚫You must not sell any digital goods or services through your apps.
⚠️If you later release a paid app, the waiver is automatically revoked.
🔄Eligibility must be re-confirmed every year at renewal by the Account Holder.
🇪🇺

EU Bonus (2026): If your organization operates an alternative app marketplace in the European Union, the fee waiver also exempts you from the Core Technology Fee (CTF) — a new Apple charge introduced for EU alternative markets.

How to Apply

1New members: During enrollment, select the option to request a fee waiver before completing payment.
2Existing members: The Account Holder submits a fee waiver request via the Apple Developer website before membership expiration.
3Apple reviews the request and may contact you for additional supporting documentation.
4Apple reserves the right to determine eligibility at its discretion.

Important Notes

📝

The name used during enrollment must exactly match official ID documentation — no aliases or company names are allowed in the personal section.

🚫

Driver's licenses are not accepted for identity verification in Mexico. An alternate form of government-issued photo ID is required.

🏷️

Organizations without a D-U-N-S number can request one at no cost through Dun & Bradstreet. This is a mandatory requirement for organization enrollment.

📬

Enrollment approval is communicated via email. Applicants must monitor their inbox for next steps after submitting their application.

💳

The annual membership subscription is a paid requirement to complete enrollment and access all Apple developer resources and distribution tools.

🎓

The fee waiver does not apply to individuals or sole proprietors — only registered nonprofits, accredited educational institutions, and government entities qualify.

🔄

Once the fee waiver is granted, it must be renewed annually by the Account Holder confirming the organization's continued eligibility.

🚨

Publishing a paid app or enabling in-app purchases under a fee-waived account will cause the waiver to be immediately revoked.
